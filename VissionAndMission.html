Prevention is Better Than Cure

Vission:

  • Village/ Panchayat wise registration of rural practitioners.
  • Provide modern and appropriate training.
  • Provision of minimum credentials.
  • Seminars on current diseases (at least one per month).
  • Seminar on various types of diseases and their removal.
  • Awareness in healthy food habits and stress-free life.
  • Heal and prevent lifestyle diseases.
  • Provision of area-wise online blood banks.
  • Team building for emergency services.
  • Stopping the use of various high-potency chemical medicines.

Mission:

  • To develop one male and one female health worker in every village.
  • To establish a therapy centre in every village.
  • Learn holistic nutrition merged with medical science.
  • To create awareness for maintaining good health through food habits and lifestyle.
  • Building a mental affinity towards practicing yoga at home.
  • Spreading the positive effects of AYUSH medicine at home.
  • To sensitize parents about the good health of children.
  • What is hygiene? To make everyone aware of this concept.
  • Creating a pollution-free environment.
  • To create a prejudice-free society.
